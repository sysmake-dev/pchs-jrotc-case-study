Palmetto Coast Leadership Academy

Building leadership, discipline, and responsibility to motivate young people to be better citizens.

Our Brigade

The Palmetto Coast High School Leadership Academy provides students with opportunities to develop leadership skills, build discipline, and take on real responsibility within a structured environment.

Cadets participate in training, events, academics, and team-based activities that prepare them for future success.

Special Teams

Our leadership academy offers multiple teams that allow students to develop specialized skills, compete with other schools, make new friends, and learn life skills that will carry outside of the program.

From handling an air rifle to getting certified in drone piloting, Palmetto Coast High Leadership Academy offers a variety of opportunities for cadets to succeed.
