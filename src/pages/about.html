About PCHS Leadership Academy

Developing leadership, citizenship, character, and responsibility through service, teamwork, and personal growth.

Our Mission

The Palmetto Coast High School Leadership Academy is committed to developing students into responsible citizens and capable leaders through leadership education, teamwork, community service, and personal accountability.

The progam encourages cadets to challenge themselves, develop confidence, and build skills that extend beyond the classroom and into everyday life.

What Cadets Learn

Leadership

Developing communication, decision-making, and problem-solving skills.

Responsibility

Developing accountability, organization, and professional conduct.

Teamwork

Working together to accomplish goals and support fellow cadets.

Citizenship

Understanding service, respect, and contributions to the community.

Discipline

Building self-control, time management, and personal responsibility.

Opportunities for Cadets

Cadets have opportunities to participate in a variety of activities throughout the school year, including leadership positions, community service projects, academic opportunities, special teams, school events, field trips, and leadership experiences.

The program encourages every cadet to become involved and discover their strengths through participation and service.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Doing what is right even when no one is watching.

Respect

Treating others with professionalism and dignity.

Service

Contributing positively to the school and community.

Excellence

Striving for continuous growth and improvement.
