Special Teams

Explore the teams and activities that allow cadets to develop new skills, compete, and represent the academy.

Drill Team

The Drill Team focuses on precision, discipline, teamwork, and military bearing through coordinated marching and ceremonial routines.

Color Guard

The Color Guard proudly represents the academy during school functions and community events while demonstrating professionalism and respect for national traditions.

JLAB (Academic Team)

The Junior Leadership and Academic Bowl challenges cadets in leadership knowledge, academics, and teamwork through regional and national competition.

Marksmanship Team

The Marksmanship Team develops focus, discipline, and responsibility through precision air rifle competition and safety training.
